Gautam Buddha University was the dream project of former chief minister of Uttar Pradesh, Ms Mayawati.
[citation needed] The university was established by the Uttar Pradesh Government under the Gautam Buddha
University Act of 2002 and began its first academic session in 2008. The university is fully funded by NOIDA
